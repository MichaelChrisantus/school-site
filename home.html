Who We Are

St. Anthony's School is a vibrant faith-centered community that provides academic excellence rooted in Gospel values. Our students are nurtured to grow intellectually, spiritually, and socially in a safe and inclusive environment.

We offer programs from Pre-K through 8th grade and emphasize leadership, service, and character development.

Learn More

Welcome to St. Anthony's School

At St. Anthony's School, we are dedicated to nurturing the minds and spirits of every student. Our mission is to provide a strong academic foundation, guided by faith and values, in a loving and inclusive community.

Whether you are a current family or new to our school, we welcome you to explore, learn, and grow with us.
